Hallowed Hills Police Department

Law Enforcement Division

Welcome to the Hallowed Hills Police Department application portal. Please review all requirements before applying. The HHPD is looking for professional, mature, and dedicated individuals who are willing to serve the community with integrity, patience, and respect.

Requirements

  • Must be 17 years of age or older.
  • Must have a professional roleplay character name.
  • Must not have any ban, major punishment, or active player report within the last 30 days.
  • Must be able to remain calm and professional during high-pressure situations.
  • Must agree to follow all Hallowed Hills Police Department documents, rules, policies, and SOPs.
  • Must be respectful toward civilians, other departments, staff, and fellow officers.
  • Must be willing to complete training before operating as a full officer.

Application Process

Applications must be completed honestly and with effort. Low-effort, incomplete, disrespectful, or unserious applications may be denied.

Do not contact Police Command, supervisors, or staff asking for status updates. If your application is accepted, you will be contacted through Discord.

If you are not contacted within 2 weeks, assume your application was denied. You may reapply later if applications are still open.

Department Expectations

Members of the Hallowed Hills Police Department are expected to represent the server with professionalism. Officers should use good judgment, follow chain of command, communicate clearly, and create fair roleplay for everyone involved.
